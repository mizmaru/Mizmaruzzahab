Fried Rice

Ingridients

  1. Cup of Uncooked Long-grain rice
  2. 2 Table Spoon Vegetable Oil
  3. Large Onion
  4. 2 Cloves
  5. 2 Teaspoon Greated Ginger
  6. 2 Teaspoon Greated crucumin

Preparation

  • Heat the oil gently with a moderate heat until the onion turns to golden color. Add onion Garlc and ginge. Cook until you start percieving the AROMA
  • Add Crucumin, paparika, salt and pepper and stir well
  • Add Tomato Paste
  • Add Rice and Stir gently
  • Reduce the heat and leave it for 20 minutes
